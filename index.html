Works

ARNERGY

Design, Front-End Development

Arnergy Solar Limited is a company providing portable electricity for Nigerians using Solar panels. The work was to redesign their website to create a more accessible, simple and consistent experience for their users. As part of the redesign, we discovered that solar panels were esoteric to most users. This allowed us focus more on the services offered by the company, and benefits that come having a solar inverter as opposed to focusing on the technical details of the device. Overall, the redesign covered the landing page, about page, and 4 other pages.

ARNERGY IOT DASHBOARD

Design

As part of delivering a unique and complete experience for people who purchased Solar Inverters manufactured by Arnergy, there was a need to design a dashboard that provided users with information about their solar panels and inverters, and allowed them make payments for their services too. This engagement was particularly tricky because generally, solar panels and inverters are highly technical devices so we had to be sure of what to present. The approach taken was extensively interview users to have their sense of what they needed to know, this guided what data was presented on the customers dashboard.

SHUTTLERS

Design

Shuttlers is a mobility business that uses technology to help shuttle professionals around Lagos using buses. Shuttlers have a web and mobile app where users are required to book shuttles. As part of my design accelerator program, a colleague and I redesigned the book experience for shuttlers for their web application. The need for a redesign became obvious as research showed the most users relied on the booking channel made available on WhatsApp as opposed on the web and mobile apps. Overall, the redesign focused on the optimizing routes for users who used the platform repeatedly while allowing for easy discoverability of routes for new users.
